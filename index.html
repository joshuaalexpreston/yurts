About Us

We build and deploy custom yurts for Burning Man attendees—delivering and installing before you arrive, and removing everything after your camp departs.

Each yurt is designed for extreme desert conditions: insulated, reflective, and structurally sound. Your campsite experience should be about art, community, and comfort—not sun, dust, or setup stress.
